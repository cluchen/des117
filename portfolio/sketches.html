Challenge Statement:

Sketch a person, place, or thing using the Illustrator pen tool.

Solution Statement:

This was my first time sketching on Illustrator, and it proved to be more difficult than imagined! I used a tablet connected to the computer for these sketches, so that I could draw more detailed lines and could add more definition. I purposefully sketched in with many many lines; it added depth and created shadowing into whatever box I was drawing to bring it to life.
