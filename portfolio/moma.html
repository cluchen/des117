Challenge Statement:

Create a mock poster showcasing the work of James Rosenquist, a leader in the arts as an exhibition poster.

Solution Statement:

I created this poster to be very similar to the last, but instead add the MoMA text above in a clear way. I decided on using an overall aligned feel for this poster, as his artwork tends to be just boxy strips of art--mimicking this, the text alignment is shaped in a very specific and equal margined way. The three pieces chosen for the artwork are vastly different and made in different periods of his life; they showcase the retrospective aspect of this exhibition.
