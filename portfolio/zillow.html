Challenge Statement:

Create and design a mockup ad for any company, showcasing their features in a visually appealing way through Illustrator.

Solution Statement:

The way I brought this to be “visually appealing” was by using bright and eye-catching colors (green, yellow, gray, brown, blue) that are all soft shades in their respective color. This makes the colors and overall visual easy on the eyes, while still portraying a bright feel. I also wanted to showcase what zillow.com is known for--finding houses! I’ve created 3 houses (that are quite similar to one another), thus allowing zillow.com be the focus of the visual.
