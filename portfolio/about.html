Hello Hello!

My name is Cynthia, and as said before, I'm a second year Cognitive Science Major, Computer Science Minor at the University of California, Davis!

Having grown up in the Bay Area, I've always been exposed to the upcomings of technology and seen it rise just within my lifetime. Most people around me back home work within the field of tech, and are the forerunners in modernizing our world, developing new products and ideas. It's always been so cool to see the impact they have on the world they've built! I have always wanted to help create empathy in the tech workforce. I want to make products that bridge the gap between users and developers through UX Design & Research; that when a product is made, it's with a useable purpose.
