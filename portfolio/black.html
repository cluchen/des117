Challenge Statement:

Produce a black and white photo with grain.

Solution Statement:

I decided to use a slight grain on the photo instead of going all the way, as then it’s still clear for the viewer and easy to see. I purposefully put the sign “MOTEL” right in the middle of the photo; everything in the picture surrounds this motel, so that there’s still a “figure of focus” in the picture. The palm trees add a nice background view, but doesn't take up the main focus.
